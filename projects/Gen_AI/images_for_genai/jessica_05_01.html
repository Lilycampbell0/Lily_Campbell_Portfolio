Client News & Developments

Recent financial results for Hasbro have been mixed. While cost-cutting measures led to a profit beat in the third quarter of 2024, overall sales dropped, reflecting weak toy demand. The company's digital gaming segment has shown strength, offsetting some of the weakness in traditional toys. However, previous quarters showed significant revenue declines, including a 23.2% drop in February 2024, and a 16% decrease in the second quarter of 2025. The industry is facing challenges, with Hasbro and Mattel both experiencing slowdowns in sales of certain product lines. Importantly, significant tariffs imposed on goods from China in 2025 have impacted Hasbro's profitability and product offerings.

Portfolio & Market Overview

Jessica Palmer's portfolio includes holdings in several major companies, reflecting a diversified investment strategy. Recent performance has been mixed across these holdings.
